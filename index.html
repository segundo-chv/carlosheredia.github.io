Flagship work / Trabajo destacado

I turned a broken workflow into an AI engine

The problem
Our partnerships team was stuck in a slow, manual workflow — proposals, evaluations, and reports each took hours of digging through scattered information. With a lean team, we couldn't chase enough leads or move fast enough to scale.
What I built
As a non-engineer, I designed and built an AI-powered operating layer for the entire partnerships function — a central Claude system the team uses every day to research, write, report, and close.
The result
Work that took hours now takes minutes, the whole team runs on it daily, and it directly powered the deals I sourced and closed — from $357K in signed contracts to the 211 LA partnership projected to generate millions.
Hours → minutes
Gathering the information to draft a proposal or complete an evaluation went from hours of manual digging to a couple of minutes — for every person on the team.
The Core

A central Claude workspace — the team's brain

The foundation: a Claude project I build and maintain, loaded with all of our partnership information. Instead of hunting through scattered docs, anyone can ask Claude directly and navigate the team's entire knowledge base — and everything else runs on top of it.

Generate

Proposals, evaluations & custom emails

The team generates partnership proposals, fills out evaluation documents, and drafts tailored outreach emails through Claude — consistent, fast, and grounded in our real data.

Integrate

HubSpot, run inside Claude

I wired our CRM directly into the workspace, so the team can run HubSpot from within Claude — no context-switching between tools to manage contacts, deals, and pipeline.

Report

Automated reporting via Claude skills

I built our automated reporting systems as Claude skills wired into our connectors, generating network and pipeline reports polished enough to be approved by our executive team.

Scale

BDA consultant program management

I manage a program of Business Development Associates — outside consultants who connect us to new sales leads — and use Claude to coordinate and scale the program's output.
